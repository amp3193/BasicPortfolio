About Me

I am currently learning web development and this is my profile. I have a Bachelor's degree in Communications from Florida State. I wanted to get into Public Relations but never got past the mailroom. I was awesome at sorting mail though and cleaning the breakroom but never really got to help the business stand out in the media. I took a haitus to raise a family and after that time the public relations game had changed so since I would have to relearn a field I decided to make it software instead.
I taught myself QA. I took Udemy courses on software testing and took the ISTQB to be certified in software testing. I worked for almost 4 years testing software as a Quality Assurance Engineer. I found some interesting bugs and really enjoyed mobile and API testing. However, I was bored. I found myself not really interested in QA. I didn't want to just test software anymore. I want to make software, awesome software.
I live in Kennesaw, GA with my husband Andy and our three children. I love sewing and have made some very cool costumes and some decent clothes. There is typically an unfinished jigsaw puzzle out in my house; I just finished my second 3000 piece puzzle! I love cats a lot, I am very thankful my husband stops me from becoming a crazy cat lady!
